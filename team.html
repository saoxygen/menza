Russell Paul

Russell Paul is a seasoned sports executive with experience in leading bids for significant sporting events. He has held senior management and leadership roles in South Africa and internationally. Some of his more notable positions include CEO of the South African bid to host the 2027 FIFA Women's World Cup, COO of the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022, and Acting CEO of the South African Football Association (SAFA). Russell has also served as Divisional Manager of Competitions for the 2010 FIFA World Cup LOC South Africa. He has been involved in organizing various international tournaments such as the Nike Premier Cup, the Meridian Cup, the CAF African Women's Championship, the African Youth Championship, the Africa Cup of Nations, the African Nations Championship, the Africa Futsal Cup of Nations, and the COSAFA Cup.
