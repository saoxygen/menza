WHO ARE WE ?

Menza Group International ( “MGI” ) is a consultancy firm with a unique focus on mega sports events, tournament operations, ceremonies, fan parks, legacy programms, stadia management, as well as Professional League and Club management.

Since the firm’s inception in 2010, we have consistently grown while staying true to our core values. We engage and contract the services of expert consultants, all leaders in their particular field of expertise, depending on the nature and the scope of work required for each specific project. These experts consultants add value to the firm by bringing their specialised knowledge and expertise to bear on each project. In short, anything related to the Business of Sport is incorporated under our umbrella.

MGI has solid experience in the management and delivery of major international sporting events, including inter alia the 2010 FIFA World Cup in South Africa, the African Cup of Nations, the Rugby World Cup, the Cricket World Cup, as well as significant domestic sporting events on the African Continent.

We are deeply passionate and committed to working together with our Clients, by providing expert advice and solutions to them within the broader defines of the Sports Industry in general, and sporting events / tournaments in particular. Our dealings with our Clients incorporate and prioritise cost effective solutions to ensure they achieve their objectives. In this way, we strive to leave a lasting and sustainable legacy in host territories, for the ultimate benefit of the Local communities.
